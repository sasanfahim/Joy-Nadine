Joy Nadine is a 27 year old songwriter, vocalist, pianist and music educator from north London. This LIPA graduate (The Liverpool Institute for performing Arts) has over a decade of performance and writing experience, with music being the heart of everything she does.

She is a versatile songwriter and performer. Despite being a classically trained pianist, she prides herself in being able to create catchy pop/dance tracks. Joy is currently collaborating with a number of songwriters and producers, performing with her band the Sound Party, as well as being a qualified Secondary School music teacher in a north London Secondary School.

Some personal achievements include: Finalist for Holloway Art Festival's Singer-songwriter competition and a semi-finalist in the UK songwriting competition. East Barnet Festival main stage 2013 and 2014, High Barnet Festival 2014, Mayor of London Gigs big busk competition 2014 and 2018, Media Rocks final 2017.
