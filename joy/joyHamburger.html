Joy Nadine

Songwriter, Vocalist, Pianist, Music Educator

company Copyright symbol; 2018


Joy is a 26 year old songwriter, vocalist, pianist and music educator from north London. She has over a decade of performance and writing experience, with music being the heart of everything she does

Education/training:

Joy is a classically trained pianist, with grade 8 piano, GCSE music, Alevel Music and Alevel music technology. She studied her music degree at the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts (LIPA), achieving a 2:1 in 2013. She is also a qualified secondary school music teacher, with a PGCE in secondary music teaching from Middlesex University, where in 2016 she graduated as an 'outstanding' teacher.

Music Teacher:

She has been a full time secondary school music teacher for 3 years and has been at a north London secondary school for the past 2 years. Subjects/courses taught include RSL Music Performance, RSL Music Technology, GCSE music, as well as KS3 drama. She has also worked as a freelance teacher for 'Fixation Theatre Company'. At Fixation, she has worked as a singing teacher, teaching students musical theatre songs, as well as facilitating songwriting workshops. She has been a musical director for fixation shows as well as productions at her school.

Songwriter:

Joy has been writing for many years and particularly enjoys co-writing. Her genre sits in the pop/dance genre although she is not afraid to collaborate and experiment with different styles of music. She is currently working on a number co-writes as well as working on briefs for syncs on her own. Some personal songwriting achievements include: Finalist for Holloway Art Festival's Singer-songwriter competition 2014 and a semi-finalist in the UK songwriting competition 2014.

Performer:

She performs as a solo artist as well as with her band 'The Sound Party'. Some of her performances have included: East Barnet Festival main stage 2013 and 2014, High Barnet Festival 2014, Mayor of London Gigs big busk competition 2014 and 2018, Media Rocks final 2017, as well as various pubs, and events around London
